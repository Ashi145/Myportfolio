Designed an end-to-end design system for a mobile-first fintech startup targeting East African millennials. The challenge was making complex financial data feel approachable and even delightful — without sacrificing clarity or trust signals.

AFROBEATS FESTIVAL

A vibrant visual campaign for a three-day music festival in Kampala. The brief called for energy and cultural pride — the response was a layered collage system mixing Kente-inspired geometry, bold display typography, and photographic halftones.

Campaign spanned 8 poster formats, social media assets (80+ templates), stage backdrops, lanyard and wristband design, and event programmes.

LÚMEN MAGAZINE

A biannual architecture and interiors publication requiring a design system that could flex across long-form features, photography spreads, and data-driven infographics. The grid is strict where it needs to be, broken where it shouldn't be.

Three complete issues designed — 160 pages each — with custom section dividers, pull quote typesetting, and a cover series using embossed foil printing simulation.

Packaging Illustration Branding

OKELLO SPIRITS

Label and packaging design for a premium Ugandan gin distillery. Each bottle in the three-variant range features a hand-illustrated botanical — specific to the botanical blend inside — rendered in a one-colour engraving style that references colonial-era labels with a distinctly African lens.

The packaging won a regional PASA Award shortlist in 2024 and launched in four countries.
